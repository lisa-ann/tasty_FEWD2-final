Rainbow Salad

This salad is a burst of freshness in the midst of winter! It's a salad that'll brighten up your plate and your taste buds. Packed with flavor and packed with nutrition, it's a winner all year round!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 4-5 radishes, thinly sliced
  • Optional: a handful of fresh herbs
  • Optional topping: nuts for crunch
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp maple syrup (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Preparation
  1. In a large salad bowl, layer the mixed greens.
  2. Add sliced bell pepper, cucumber, radish, and diced avocado on top.
  3. If using, sprinkle with fresh herbs and seeds/nuts.
  4. In a small jar or bowl, whisk together the dressing ingredients until well combined.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
